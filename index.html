About the Book

James, a brilliant scientist, works for Alpheus, a wealthy multinational technology company. He is a quantum computing expert, but before he can hand over his work to the company, he's talked into stealing the idea by a mysterious program manager with a questionable background.

Set in a world of high-stakes corporate espionage, quantum computing, and ethical dilemmas, Quantum Eschaton will keep you on the edge of your seat. A gripping tale of intrigue, betrayal, and a race against time, this book is perfect for fans of techno-thrillers and suspenseful dramas.
